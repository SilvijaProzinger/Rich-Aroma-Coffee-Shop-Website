Our coffee shop

The coziest coffee shop in town.

Opened in 2017, this coffee shop is a product of long lasting passion for coffee, good music and pleasant company. Founded by Melanie McCollins and her friend Josh Levi, baristas with more than 6 years of experience, our coffee shop is your number one place for relaxation and good coffee.

Experienced baristas

Melanie McCollins and Josh Levi both have more than 6 years of experience as baristas and continous passion for coffee and perfect service. With hundreds of favorite customers we are proud to say we are someone's best cup of coffee.
